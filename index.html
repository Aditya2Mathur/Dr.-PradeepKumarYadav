about us

Experienced Orthopedic Surgeon in Shahjahanpur

Dr. Pradeep Yadav is a well-known Orthopedic Surgeon in Shahjahanpur with more than 10 years of experience in treating bone and joint problems. He has performed 500+ Hip Replacements, 150+ Knee Replacements, and thousands of fractures treatment helping patients walk freely without pain. After completing his MBBS from KGMU Lucknow and MS Orthopedics from LLRM Meerut, he worked in leading hospitals including BKL Max Hospital, New Delhi, where he assisted in more than 5000 joint replacement surgeries. Presently, he is the Head of Orthopedic Unit at Satyanand Hospital, Shahjahanpur, and also teaches as an Assistant Professor in medical colleges. His focus is on providing safe, reliable, and advanced treatment for patients suffering from joint pain, fractures, spine trauma, and pediatric bone problems. Patients from Shahjahanpur, Bareilly, Farrukhabad, and Hardoi trust him for quality orthopedic care.
